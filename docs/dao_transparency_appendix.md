## DAO & Treasury Transparency

Rhythm Master operates a season-based economy funded primarily by Season Pass sales.

### Revenue Sources
- Season Pass sales (primary)
- Optional SSN practice fees
- Marketplace royalties (future)
- Sponsorships (future)

### Allocation Model
Revenue is split transparently using a public calculator:

- 55% Player Rewards
- 15% Artist Pool
- 15% DAO Treasury
- 15% Operations & Development

### Public Calculator
All season economics are modeled using a publicly accessible
Google Sheets calculator, allowing players and artists to:

- Verify prize pool sizes
- Stress-test season outcomes
- Understand token allocations

Final reward distributions are published at season end and verifiable on-chain.

### No Investment Disclaimer
Season Passes, SSN, WUF, and NFTs are utility assets only and
do not represent equity, profit rights, or investment contracts.
